A Short Biography

I am an observational astronomer and I currently work as a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California in Santa Barbara. I am a member of the ENIGMA group led by Joe Hennawi and I am also part of the Pan-STARRS team quasars led by Fabian Walter. Previously, I was postdoctoral researcher at the Max-Planck-Institut für Astronomie in Heidelberg. I did my PhD at the Università degli Studi dell'Insubria and my undergraduate studies at the Università degli Studi di Milano-Bicocca (with some months at the Universität Zürich).
